Quest 2 · Overview

Goal: compare clean energy technologies and recommend the best mix for Capital City by 2050.

  • Scope: Solar PV, Wind, Small Hydro, Geothermal, Biomass, Storage (BESS/PHS)
  • Criteria: Technical Feasibility · Potential Benefits · Challenges (with mitigation)
  • Approach: City context + resource fit + grid needs; concise, evidence-based bullets
  • Outcome: Portfolio that advances 100% clean energy by 2050 with reliable operations

Recommendations (Preview)

  • Primary: Rooftop/Carport & Utility-scale Solar PV + BESS
  • Complementary: Onshore Wind at ridgelines; In-conduit Small Hydro (waterworks)
  • Situational: Geothermal (where gradient allows), Biomass only from waste streams
